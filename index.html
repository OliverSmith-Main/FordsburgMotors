Not Just Fitters.
Real Mechanics.

Fordsburg Motors was built by brothers Ilyaas and Tarek with one goal: transparent, high-end mechanical work without the dealership tax.

We specialize in the heavy stuff. Got an electrical gremlin? Need a complex engine teardown? Or maybe you just need a clean space to plug in your dual-monitor setup and code while we sort out your major service. We got you covered.
